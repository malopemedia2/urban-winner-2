About TX Theatre

TX Theatre is a modern performance venue dedicated to delivering unforgettable live entertainment experiences. From dramatic stage plays to musical productions, cultural showcases, and Broadway-inspired performances, we bring creativity and storytelling to life.

Gallery

Our Objectives

• To promote both local and international theatrical talent.

• To create a space where creativity and performance thrive.

• To deliver world-class entertainment experiences.

• To support youth development in performing arts.

• To strengthen the theatre community across Tembisa and beyond.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading theatre brand known for innovation, diversity, and artistic excellence.Our Vision – TX Theatre To become a leading and transformative theatre institution in South Africa, celebrated for creativity, artistic excellence, and community engagement. We aim to provide a platform where local talent can thrive, stories can be told authentically, and audiences can experience theatre that inspires, educates, and unites. TX Theatre envisions a future where the performing arts elevate culture, nurture young artists, and bridge communities through meaningful, immersive, and impactful performances.
